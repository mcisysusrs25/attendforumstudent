Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up for AttendForum?

You can sign up for AttendForum using your university email. Just click on the "Sign Up" button and follow the instructions.

How do I mark my attendance?

Simply scan the QR code provided by your professor during class. The system will automatically verify your location and mark your attendance.

Can I view my attendance history?

Yes, you can view both current and past attendance records in the "Session Tracking" section of your dashboard.

Location Access Policy

1. Collection of Location Data

To verify attendance, AttendForum collects location data when you scan the QR code in class. This data is used solely for attendance tracking purposes and is not shared with third parties.

2. User Consent

By using AttendForum, you consent to the collection of your location data as described in this policy. You may revoke this consent at any time by disabling location services in your device settings.

3. Data Retention

Location data is retained only for the duration necessary to verify your attendance. We do not store your location history beyond this purpose.
